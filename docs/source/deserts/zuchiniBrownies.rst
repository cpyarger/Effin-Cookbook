Zucchini Brownies
==============================
---------
Kim Smith
---------
.. toctree::

.. list-table:: Ingredients
   :header-rows: 0

   * - 2 cups grated Zucchini
     - 1 1/2 cup sugar
   * - 1/2 cup oil
     - 2 cups flour
   * - 1 tsp salt
     - 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
   * - 1/3 cup cocoa
     - 2 tsp vanilla
   * - (optional) 1 egg for more moist Brownies
     -

1. Mix all Ingredients in bowl
2. Pour into oven safe pan
3. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes
