Easy Peanut Butter Fudge
========================
.. toctree::

.. list-table:: Ingredients
   :header-rows: 0

   * - 1 Can Vanilla Frosting
     - 1 jar Peanut butter

1. Microwave both for 1 minute each.
2. Pour both into bowl and mix thouroughly 
3. Pour into buttered pan
4. Cool completely before you cut
