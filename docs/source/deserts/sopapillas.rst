Sopapillas
==========
.. toctree::

.. list-table:: Ingredients
   :header-rows: 0

   * - 2 Cups flour
     - 1 Tbsp baking soda
   * - 1/2 tsp salt
     - 1 Tbsp Shortening
   * - 2/3 cup lukewarm water
     -

1. Stir together flour, baking soda, and salt
2. Cut in Shortening
3. Gradually add water while stirring with fork
4. Kneed into a ball
5. Cut dough in 2
6. Let stand 10 minutes
7. Roll out dough onto Floured surface to make a 12 1/2" by 10" rectangle
8. Cut into 2 1/2" sqiares
9. Drop into 425F deep fryer
10. Turn frequently until golden brown
