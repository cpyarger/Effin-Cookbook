Peanut Butter Fudge
===================
.. toctree::

.. list-table:: ingredients
   :header-rows: 0

   * - 3/4 Cup milk
     - 1 Cup Sugar
   * - 1 cup brown sugar, packed
     - 3 rounded Tbsp peanut butter


1. Cook in sauce pan stir constantly till mixture foams soft 
2. Remove from stove and stir in canilla and peanut butter
3. Stir while cooling when stuf and light color pack into greased 8x8 pan
