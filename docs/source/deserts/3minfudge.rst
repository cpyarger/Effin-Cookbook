3 Minute Fudge
==============
.. toctree::

.. list-table:: Ingredients
   :header-rows: 0

   * - 2 cups chocolate chips
     - 14 oz can condensed milk
   * - 2 tsp vanilla
     - 1/4 cups raisins

1. Add chips and condensed milk to bowl.
2. Microwave 3 minutes on high.
3. Add vanilla and raisins.
4. Pour into 8x8 in foil lined pan.
