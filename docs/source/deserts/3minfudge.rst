3 Minute Fudge
==============
.. toctree::

.. list-table:: Ingredients
   :header-rows: 0

   * - 2 cups chocolate chips
     - 14 oz can condensed milk
   * - 2 tsp vanilla
     - 1/4 cups raisins

Add chips and condensed milk to bowl.
Microwave 3 minutes on high.
Add vanilla and raisins.
Pour into 8x8 in foil lined pan.
