Blueberry Muffins
=================
.. toctree::

.. list-table:: Ingredients
   :header-rows: 0

   * - 6 Tbsp Shortening
     - 3/4 cup Sugar
   * - 1/4 tsp vinegar
     - 1 egg
   * - 3/4 cup milk
     - 1/2 tsp vanilla
   * - 2 cups flour
     - 3/4 tsp baking soda
   * - 1 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
     - 

1. Cream together Shortening, sugar, and vinegar.
2. Add egg vanilla and milk
3. Mix together flour soda and cream of tartar
4. Add Together both mixes.
5. Pour into paper lined cupcake pan, filling only halfway
6. bake at 400F for 16 minutes.
