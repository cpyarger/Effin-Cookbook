Sugar Loaf Coffee Cake
======================
.. toctree::


.. list-table:: ingredients
    :header-rows: 0

    * - 2 1/2 Cups flour
      - 1 1/4 Cups Sugar
    * - 3 /2 tsp baking Powder
      - 1 tsp salt
    * -  tsp nutmeg
      - 1 tsp cinnamon
    * - 1/2 tsp cloves
      - 1/2 tsp allspice
    * - 3/4 cup Shortening
      - 1 cup raisins
    * - 1 cup milk or buttermilk
      - 1 egg beaten

Heat oven to 375F.
grease a square 9x9in pan.
mix dry ingredients well in bowl.
cut shortening till it looks like oatmeal.
set aside 1 cup for top.
add raisins.
mix milk and eggstir dry ingredients  until just a little moist.
pour into pan.
sprinkle reserved mixture over top.
bake 40-45 minutes.
serve warm.
