Sugar Loaf Coffee Cake
======================
.. toctree::


.. list-table:: ingredients
    :header-rows: 0

    * - 2 1/2 Cups flour
      - 1 1/4 Cups Sugar
    * - 3 /2 tsp baking Powder
      - 1 tsp salt
    * -  tsp nutmeg
      - 1 tsp cinnamon
    * - 1/2 tsp cloves
      - 1/2 tsp allspice
    * - 3/4 cup Shortening
      - 1 cup raisins
    * - 1 cup milk or buttermilk
      - 1 egg beaten

1. Heat oven to 375F.
2. Grease a square 9x9in pan.
3. Mix dry ingredients well in bowl.
4. Cut shortening till it looks like oatmeal.
5. Set aside 1 cup for top.
6. Add raisins.
7. Mix milk and egg
8. Stir in dry ingredients until just a little moist.
9. pour into pan.
10. sprinkle reserved mixture over top.
11. bake 40-45 minutes.
12. serve warm.
